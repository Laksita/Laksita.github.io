About me

I’m deeply passionate about cultural arts, community involvement, and creating meaningful experiences through leadership and collaboration. At the University of Louisville, I’ve been actively involved in several major campus events hosted by the Indian Student Association (ISA)—including serving as Décor Chair for Jalsa, UofL’s largest Diwali celebration, and Head Liaison for Derby City Dhoom, a national Bhangra and Fusion dance competition held on campus.

Classical arts are also a huge part of my life. I’ve trained in Bharatanatyam for many years and recently began learning Carnatic music, which has allowed me to stay connected to my cultural roots through both dance and singing. Whether I’m performing, organizing, mentoring, or volunteering, I genuinely enjoy bringing people together and celebrating community, culture, and creativity.
